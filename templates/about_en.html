What is UPVC? Upvc is the raw material for making door and window profiles, which consists of the abbreviation Unplasticized Poly Vinyl Chloride. This material is a type of modern thermoplastic and consists of the main derivatives of crude oil and salt. In the UPVC production process by adding different materials such as: Impact Modifier Heat Stabilizers Fillers Processing Aids Internal & External Labricants Pigment By combining the above materials with PVC powder, a material (UPVC) is obtained, which is a new combination of PVC raw material, but due to the different physical properties of this new material, it is called a non-plastic material. Advantages of using UPVC doors and windows No need for paint Quick and easy installation Easy maintenance and cleaning Has a long useful life Sound, heat and cold insulation Resistant to rot and rust High flexibility and variety in construction Unemployed in case of fire Energy savings of up to 40% Resistant to wind, dust, water and rain
